How to Find Commercial Real Estate For Sale

October 16, 2020 Brit

LoopNet is a great way to find commercial real estate

When it comes to searching for commercial real estate, there is no shortage of resources out there. There are plenty of commercial real estate listing platforms that focus on certain types of properties or geographical regions. But if you want to cast the biggest net possible and use a trusted and industry-respected source, we recommend using LoopNet.

What is LoopNet?

LoopNet is an online commercial real estate listing platform. It is part of CoStar Group, which is a global leader in marketing commercial real estate online. The website boasts that CoStar has, across all of its platforms, had 1.3 billion visits to their sites in the past year, has marketed 1.1 million properties, leased 20 million properties, sold 1 million, through the 300,000 clients that have marketed their properties using CoStar and LoopNet.

How do I use LoopNet to find commercial real estate for sale?

Before searching LoopNet, it’s a good idea to have a plan in place for what you are looking for. The three things you should know before you start is

1) If you want to lease or buy

2) What type of building you want

3) Location.

LoopNet allows you to narrow down your search using these primary categories. For location, you can even narrow your search down by state, county, zip code, even street and intersection. 

There are a few other options for searching for buildings as well, such as square footage, size, and of course, price. As you browse the properties, you will find some listings that have been neglected or that are inaccurate. Once you start zeroing in on some promising properties, be sure to check with the listing agent to make sure you’re not wasting time on a bad listing. 

In the end, the best way to figure out LoopNet is to get on it and play around. It’s an intuitive system that you should be able to navigate with ease fairly quickly, and soon, your search will be rewarded!
