What Should a Commercial Property Inspection Cost?

We believe in transparent and simple pricing. Yes, every property is different and the other guys might build their commercial inspection bids based on square footage, property elements, and even a percentage of the sale price. Complex pricing just leads to confusion and we prefer to keep the inspection process as simple as possible. After all, the inspection and report are complicated enough. We look to achieve a seamless and effortless scheduling and payment process by keeping simple what should be simple.

To determine pricing for any commercial inspection we take into account how long the property will take to inspect and how many inspectors will be required to complete the project in that timeline.

Are Commercial Inspectors Charging Too Much?

That’s not for us to say. What we can say is that we believe everyone should pay a fair amount for a service provided and that the service provider ought to be completely transparent about how they build their pricing. At Focus Building Inspections we value transparency, integrity, and fairness. From the start we have sought to leave real estate better than we found it. We’re just doing our part in bringing change to “our piece of real estate” within commercial inspections in our neck of the woods.
