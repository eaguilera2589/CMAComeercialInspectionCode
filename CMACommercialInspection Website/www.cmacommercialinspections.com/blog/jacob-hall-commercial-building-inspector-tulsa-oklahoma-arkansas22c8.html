Please Welcome Our Newest Commercial Inspector

January 5, 2021 Brit

Commercial Inspector, Jacob Hall Has Joined Focus Building Inspections

After a successful start in residential real estate inspections with Forever Home Inspection, Jacob was recruited to join Focus Building Inspections as a commercial building inspector.

Prior to being a building inspector, Jacob spent many years working as a carpenter in dozens of homes and apartments in downtown Tulsa. After completing his Master’s degree, he transitioned to the construction management industry. His background in construction prepared him well for the attention to detail required for inspecting commercial properties.

He has completed 100’s of residential inspections and has impressed his clients with his ability to connect with people during what can be an anxiety-ridden, but exciting time. He possesses a keen ability at helping others feel calm with his professional and kind demeanor while delivering a high level of expertise.

About Focus Building Inspections

Forever Building Inspections is a commercial property inspection company in Tulsa, Oklahoma. We work across Oklahoma and in neighboring states. Our sister company is Forever Home Inspection, which does residential inspections. We are currently one of only three CCPIA (Certified Commercial Property Inspectors Association) certified inspection companies within 200 miles of Tulsa, and the only one dedicated solely to commercial inspections.

We’ve worked with all sorts of clients and offer inspections for a range of commercial properties, including but not limited to office buildings, multifamily housing, restaurants, and warehouses. Check out our sample reports for these types of buildings.
