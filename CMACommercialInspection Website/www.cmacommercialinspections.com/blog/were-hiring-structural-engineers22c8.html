We’re Hiring Structural Engineers

December 24, 2020 Brit

Jobs for Structural Engineers in Oklahoma

We’re hiring structural engineers to join our team!

What We’re Looking For

  • We’re looking for fully qualified, already licensed and trained engineers. You will already need to have completed your degree and finished an apprenticeship with a licensed engineer. However, you do not need to have previous experience in real estate. We will guide and coach you to success in this field!

  • You need to live in the Tulsa area, although we serve in neighboring states and work across Oklahoma. Our first engineers will be in Tulsa, where we are headquartered, but we may soon be able to expand elsewhere. 

  • Our pay structure is competitive, and we will make sure you have as much work as you want and need. 

How to Apply

  • To apply, email your resume to LANCE@FOCUSBUILDINGINSPECTIONS.COM. Make sure to bcc brit@foreverhomeinspection.com as well, and put “Inquiring about structural engineer position” on the subject line (or click on our banner at the top of this page.)

About Focus Building Inspections

Forever Building Inspections is a commercial property inspection company in Tulsa, Oklahoma. We work across Oklahoma and in neighboring states. Our sister company is Forever Home Inspections, which does residential inspections. We believe in fair, transparent pricing and training our inspectors to be top-tier professionals who go above and beyond in everything they do. Focus Building Inspections is currently one of only three CCPIA (Certified Commercial Property Inspectors Association) certified inspection companies within 200 miles of Tulsa, and the only one dedicated solely to commercial inspections.

We’ve worked with all sorts of clients and offer inspections for a range of commercial properties, including but not limited to: office buildings, multifamily housing, restaurants, and warehouses. Check out our sample reports for these types of buildings.

Our team of inspectors is led by Brit and Lance Coffman, founders of Focus Building and Forever Home. Brit leads the business and administrative side, and Lance is a lead inspector. Both have deep roots in Oklahoma and Tulsa and have already made their mark on the real estate industry. If you’re looking for a rewarding, exciting career, talk to us!
