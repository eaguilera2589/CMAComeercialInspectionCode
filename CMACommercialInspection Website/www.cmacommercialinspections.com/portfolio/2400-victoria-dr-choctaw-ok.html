Who is Focus Building Inspections?

We are a commercial inspection company focusing solely on commercial properties.

At our company, we are well-versed in commercial inspections and commercial assessments. We understand that commercial properties are not just assets but also business expenses and income generators. We recognize that many clients require more than a mere building inspection report. That is why we offer professional services that help answer questions about cost-to-cure, maintenance schedules, occupancy, fire safety, evacuation, and more. These records reveal the actual cost of owning the building and provide crucial insights for investors to evaluate the property's value.
